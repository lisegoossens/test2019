Irene

For my masters project at LUCA school of arts Ghent, I wrote a story about a mermaid falling in love with a man and their child meeting the mermaid mother long after her father has passed away. I decided to tell this story entirely in images that I made in various materials and sizes. All of the images show different moments in time, the parents falling in love, the brothers living in the forest, the child coming of age, the mother's life as a mermaid... This is a selection.
