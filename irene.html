Irene

For my masters project at LUCA school of arts Ghent, I wrote a story about a child and her mermaid mother. I started with a text, but ended with only drawings. Simplified. A story told in images. They all show different moments in time: the parents falling in love, the brothers living in the forest, the child coming of age... This is a selection.
