Hi! I'm Lise Goossens (°1998), an illustrator based in Belgium. My work consists of drawings that often show nature, mysterious atmospheres or magical creatures. I'm greatly inspired by my dreams, and would love to recreate this dreamworld within my illustrations. I love to work with a mix of traditional and digital mediums, preferring to draw on paper first, then colour or animate those drawings digitally.
