Lise Goossens (°1998) is an illustrator based in Belgium. In 2020, she received her master's degree in fine art: illustration at LUCA school of arts Ghent. Some things that she is fascinated by are statues, interiors, trees, solitude and dreams. You will often see these subjects in her work.

You can contact me via email for illustration work or questions: lise@waltergoossens.com
