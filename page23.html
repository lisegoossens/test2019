Lise Goossens (1998) is an artist and illustrator based in Belgium. In 2020, she received her master's degree in fine art: illustration at LUCA school of arts Ghent. Currently she is making small works based on her imagination, memories and dreams. She likes working as spontaneously as possible, with minimal sketching. This allows her to come up with all the pictorial elements as she is painting, and thus making spontaneity, happy accidents and the mix of subconscious decisions and quick thinking possible.
