Lise Goossens (°1998) is a freelance illustrator based in Ghent, Belgium. In 2020, she received her master's degree in fine art: illustration at LUCA school of arts. She takes a lot of inspiration from her dreams, enjoys not knowing what something will end up looking like on paper, and often paints things of which she doesn’t remember exactly what they are like, what results in very naive imagery. She loves making illustrations to support a text as well as making autonomous work.

Some work that she is especially interested in would be things involving food, art, plants, women and interiors, as those are the things she likes to draw most. She is both available for commercial illustration work as well as personal commissions. She also sells prints and original works (click).

Don't hesitate to reach out via email with any questions you have!
