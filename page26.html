Hi! I'm Lise Goossens (°1998), an illustrator based in Belgium. In 2020, I received my master's degree in fine art: illustration at LUCA school of arts Ghent. My drawings often show people alone in nature, surrounded by mysterious atmospheres, and overall wading in some kind of strangeness. Some things that I am fascinated by are statues, bodies of water, trees, solitude and dreams. You will often see these subjects in my work. I like to include the unconscious by taking inspiration from my dreams, and by trying to start directly on the final drawing after letting an idea take shape in my head. By not making sketches beforehand, the drawing is as pure as possible, as connected to my brain as possible.

 Don't hesitate to contact me via email for editorial work, commissions or questions: lise@waltergoossens.com 

Visit my instagram page to see more recent work.
