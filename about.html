Lise Goossens (1998) is an illustrator and artist from Belgium, currently studying at Luca school of arts Ghent. She has always been drawing, but started painting a few years ago, and has been painting ever since. She is inspired by everything: dreams, photographs, colours, filmstills, ... Often she wakes up with an idea, which she starts to paint later on in the day with gouache.

Visit the contact page to buy work or prints, or for commissions.
