Hi! I'm Lise Goossens, an artist and illustrator based in Belgium. In 2020, I received my master's degree in fine art: illustration at LUCA school of arts Ghent. Currently  I'm making small works based on my imagination, memories and dreams. I'm also interested in doing commissioned work. The only sketching I do when starting on a personal work is in words, writing down small prompts that I come up with during the day. I look at this list of written images before I start to paint. This method allows me to come up with all the pictorial elements as I go along, and thus making spontaneity, happy accidents and the mix of subconscious decisions and quick thinking possible.


You can contact me via email for illustration work or questions: lise@waltergoossens.com
