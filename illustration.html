The survival of the weaverbirds

A5, gouache and ink


Painting inspired by weaverbirds and their nests seen in the Africamuseum in Tervuren. I was inspired by how the weaverbirds create false entrances to mislead intruders, and instantly connected this to how many animals use camouflage to mislead predators.
